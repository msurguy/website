Maks Surguy

Artist, Web Developer, Design Technologist, Writer.

Maks is a design-savvy full stack engineer turned design technologist who excels at working within multidisciplinary teams. The work that Maks especially enjoys involves technology concept development, prototyping (digital and/or physical), user research, and making software tools for artists. Originally from Kharkiv, Ukraine, Maks now lives in Washington State with his wife and two children.

Art

Maks is a digital artist and plotter enthusiast. He creates generative artworks that often come in digital and physical form. His artworks are often inspired by nature and are produced through heavy use of mathematics and coding.
